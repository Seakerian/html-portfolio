The Best Movies According to Marvin

My top 5 movies of all-time

Spider-Man Across The Spider-Verse

It has an awesome story. The soundtrack and the animation are golden.

Hereditary

It's a very eerie movie, with an awesome soundtrack and a really good plotwist at the end.

Pulp Fiction

It's a crossed stories movie, with great actors and great stories on their own.

Fight Club

It's an awesome movie, with great storytelling, and a very good plotwist. The soundtrack is supreme as well.

Suspiria

This one is a very strange yet interesting movie. It'll keep you on the edge of your seat at all times.
